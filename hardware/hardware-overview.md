# Hardware overview

NeXa RoVe is being developed around Raspberry Pi hardware and connected components. The hardware side is used to explore how an assistant can interact with the physical world while keeping safety and privacy in mind.

Public-safe hardware areas include:

- Raspberry Pi as the local hardware platform
- microphone or voice input hardware
- display and interface hardware
- sensors for status and environment experiments
- camera exploration where safe
- robotics hardware for controlled movement experiments
- power and battery status work at a general level

This page does not include exact wiring, private configuration, device-specific secrets, calibration details, or sensitive build notes.
