# Progress log

Use this template for public-safe progress updates.

## Entry template

### Date

YYYY-MM-DD

### Area

Hardware, software, UI, voice, testing, demo, documentation, or another broad area.

### What changed

A short public-safe summary of the change.

### What was learned

A short note about what the work showed or clarified.

### Public notes

Anything safe to share publicly, such as a screenshot, demo idea, limitation, or next documentation task.
